What is the website about?

Integrated Civils is a official government website that aims to connect every aspect of life through civil engineering. It is a platform for government officials, engineers, researchers, and citizens to share information, ideas, and solutions for various civil engineering challenges. The website covers topics such as structural design, geotechnical engineering, highway engineering, water resources, materials, and environmental engineering. The website also provides resources such as online courses, workshops, seminars, and publications. Integrated Civils is a website that strives to make the nation safer, smarter, and more sustainable through civil engineering.

What is Social Credit Point?

Social credit point is a system that evaluates the trustworthiness of individuals, businesses and institutions. It is based on various factors such as financial credit, legal compliance, social behavior and public reputation. Depending on their score, people and entities may face rewards or sanctions, such as easier access to loans or travel bans.
