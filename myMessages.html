What is Social Credit Point?

Right Wings

Social credit point is a system that evaluates the trustworthiness of individuals, businesses and institutions. It is based on various factors such as financial credit, legal compliance, social behavior and public reputation. Depending on their score, people and entities may face rewards or sanctions, such as easier access to loans or travel bans.

Why so Concerning?

John Malika

Social credit point is a system that assigns a score to each citizen based on their behavior and activities. It is meant to encourage good conduct and discourage bad ones. However, some people are concerned that it may violate human rights and privacy, as well as create a culture of fear and conformity. They worry that the system may be used to manipulate and oppress people who do not agree with the authorities or the majority.
